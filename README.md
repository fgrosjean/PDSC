# PDSC iOS App

![](https://mir-s3-cdn-cf.behance.net/project_modules/max_1200/56cc7c53089887.592b071958b6b.jpg)


Prados de San Cristobal needed an app that could make the life of their real estate agents much easier. Tailored for easy homes for sale tour, pitch protocol, history of the area, nearby places showcase, potential buyers registry, picture/video gallery and price quotation. After the app was release PDSC increase their real estate sales by an average of 425%.

These tools are also useful for brokers, buyers, sellers, even investors —anyone involved in real estate.
